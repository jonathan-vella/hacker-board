# InfraOps Microhack Scoring Rubric

105-point base + 25 bonus | WAF-aligned | Facilitator scoring via HackerBoard

- Project context complete — 4 points
- Functional requirements — 4 points
- NFRs (SLA, RTO, RPO) — 4 points
- Compliance identified — 4 points
- Budget stated — 4 points

## Architecture Design

- Cost estimation — 5 points
- Reliability patterns — 5 points
- Security controls — 5 points
- Scalability approach — 5 points
- Service selection — 5 points

## Implementation Quality

- Bicep compiles — 5 points
- Bicep lints clean — 5 points
- Naming conventions — 5 points
- Security hardened — 5 points
- Modular structure — 5 points

## Deployment Success

- What-If executed — 2 points
- Deployment succeeded — 4 points
- Core resources running — 2 points
- Summary documented — 2 points

## Load Testing

- Test executed — 2 points
- Targets documented — 1 points
- Results analyzed — 2 points

## Documentation

- Operations guide complete — 2 points
- Architecture documented — 2 points
- Clear formatting — 1 points

## Diagnostics

- Troubleshooting steps clear — 2 points
- Monitoring queries included — 2 points
- Incident response documented — 1 points

## Partner Showcase

- Technical communication — 3 points
- Customer engagement — 2 points
- Solution justification — 2 points
- Team collaboration — 2 points
- Active listening — 1 points
